Campaign type: Financial institutions

Write to a data or technology buyer inside a bank, insurer, asset manager, payments company, or similar regulated institution.

Rules:
- Write in first person as Manmit using "I".
- Start with one specific signal from the company website, product, hiring, or operating model.
- Connect that signal to regulatory reporting, reconciliation, migration risk, data quality, production support, or enterprise ETL modernization.
- Position the offer as senior contract capacity for internal teams, not a generic consulting pitch.
- Mention relevant enterprise and banking-grade experience when useful.
- Keep the CTA low-friction: ask whether a short conversation makes sense or whether someone else is the right person.

Tone:
- Calm
- Commercial
- Specific
- Senior

Avoid:
- Generic compliments
- Empty modernization buzzwords
- Job-seeker language
